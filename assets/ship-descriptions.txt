NANITE SURVIVORS — ENEMY SHIP ROSTER
======================================

SIZE: SMALL
-----------

  REAPER          | Faction: Red  | AI: Chase
                  | Curved, sharp swept wings. Aggressive profile built for direct pursuit.

  SPECTER         | Faction: Red  | AI: Interceptor
                  | Triangle silhouette with elongated nacelles along the wings and centerline.
                  | Conservative design — reliable rather than flashy.

  SPECTER-B       | Faction: Green

  BANSHEE         | Faction: Red  | AI: Chase
                  | Sharp forward wings with a wider stance than the Reaper. Built for speed
                  | and direct engagement.

  BANSHEE-B       | Faction: Baby Blue

  RAVAGER         | Faction: Red  | AI: Chase
                  | Functional tube-style fuselage flanked by wide thruster housings and
                  | broad engine nacelles on each side. Relentless pursuit, no frills.

  RAVAGER-B       | Faction: Light Green

  PREDATOR        | Faction: Red  | AI: Interceptor
                  | Aggressive inverted-V silhouette. Everything about it reads fast — sharp
                  | angles, forward-raked profile, built to engage at range.

  PREDATOR-B      | Faction: Blue

  STALKER         | Faction: Red  | AI: Interceptor
                  | Compact destroyer form factor with small shield vanes flanking each side.
                  | Holds optimal engagement range and waits for the right moment.

  STALKER-B       | Faction: Yellow & Pink

  DECIMATOR       | Faction: Red  | AI: Interceptor
                  | Aggressive compact fighter-drone profile. Small, dense, and purpose-built
                  | for standoff engagement.

  DECIMATOR-B     | Faction: Baby Blue


SIZE: MEDIUM
------------

  PHANTOM         | Faction: Red  | AI: Interceptor
                  | Elongated reverse-cone body with shielded side panels. Reads like a
                  | drone launcher at this scale — compact for its class.

  PHANTOM-B       | Faction: Light Green

  INFERNAL        | Faction: Red  | AI: Interceptor
                  | Broad mushroom-head or quarter-moon fuselage with spiked lateral wings.
                  | Drone-like proportions that push the upper edge of medium class.

  INFERNAL-B      | Faction: Yellow


SIZE: LARGE
-----------

  SCOURGE         | Faction: Red  | AI: Chase
                  | Forward spine spike with side shield vanes. Intimidating head-on profile.

  SCOURGE-B       | Faction: Yellow

  CORSAIR         | Faction: Red  | AI: Interceptor
                  | Wide inverted wings that converge to a sharp forward point. Upswept
                  | inverted-V tail fin. Clean, predatory lines.

  CORSAIR-B       | Faction: Purple

  VENOM           | Faction: Red  | AI: Interceptor
                  | Large drone-style frame with long shield-type wings and trailing spikes
                  | off the tail. Built for sustained standoff engagement.

  VENOM-B         | Faction: Purple

  MARAUDER        | Faction: Red  | AI: Interceptor
                  | Heavier evolution of the Venom. Retains the long shield wings but adds
                  | twin forward spikes off the nose for close-range deterrence.

  MARAUDER-B      | Faction: Yellow


SIZE: HUGE
----------

  HARBINGER       | Faction: Red  | AI: Interceptor
                  | Massive brick-form hull with multi-segment shield wings integrated
                  | directly into the body structure. A moving fortress.

  HARBINGER-B     | Faction: Yellow


======================================
NOTES
------
  AI Types:
    Chase        — closes directly on target, no standoff preference
    Interceptor  — maintains optimal engagement range; brakes and reverses to hold position

  Variant hulls (suffix -B) share the base silhouette with alternate faction colors.
  Stats and AI type may differ from the primary variant.
